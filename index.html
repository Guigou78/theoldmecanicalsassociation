The Old Mecanicals Association

Created in the summer of 2023, The Old Mecanicals Association is an association bringing together a number of real enthusiasts of vehicles and mechanical engineering in France, near Le Mans. Some of the rare and precious models date back to the 50s!

The aim of the association is to enable enthusiasts of mechanical engineering and machines to meet up around unique items and share anecdotes and advice about the various vehicles. The funds raised at the open days and various gatherings will enable the mechanics to repair and restore the vehicles, as well as expanding the collection by buying new ones!
